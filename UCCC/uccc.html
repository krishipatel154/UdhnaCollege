ABOUT COLLAGE


The Udhna Acadamy Education Trust has been registered as an education institute of Gujarat since June,1964. Our motto is "All round quality education".The student should become a valueable service provided by sharing his/her knowdleage in the socity and the gain the place at the highest pick in his/her area through technical professional skill.

PRESIDENT'S MESSAGE
Shree Bhanjibhai Patel

I am delighted to extend a warm welcome to aspiring students to join the institution managed by Udhna Academy Education Trust (Surat) based on the values of faith, knowledge and action powered by a strong will and a commitment to serve mankind. The Trust was established in 1964 with a view to nurture and support quality education and it imparts education from KG to UG level.


It has been our endeavour to provide progressive, practical and innovative education to the youth of our country. Our Management Team is putting in place numerous supportive programmes that will enhance our offer and ensure that all the students at our institutes experience quality education.
